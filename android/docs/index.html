Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 June 2025

Track Expense ("we", "our", or "us") is a local-only Android app (“the App”) that helps you record and track business expenses. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App stores all your expense entries (amount, vendor, description, date/time, project, receipt image, claimed status) locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or upload any of your personal or expense data to any server.

2. File & Image Storage

Receipt images you attach are saved in your app’s sandbox storage. When you perform a backup or export to CSV, those files are written to a folder you choose (e.g. Download/Track_Expense/data). Those CSV backups contain only the data you explicitly export—they are never shared automatically.

3. Permissions

  • Storage access: to read/write your local database, receipt images, and backup files.
  • Camera/Gallery: optional, to let you pick or capture bill photos.

No other permissions (location, contacts, microphone, internet) are requested.

4. Analytics & Ads

We do not include any analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs in the App.

5. Security

Your expense data resides only on your device. We recommend using your device’s built-in PIN or biometric lock to protect access. The App does not encrypt data at rest, so take standard precautions if you share or lose your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is rated “Everyone.” It does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will appear here with an updated “Last updated” date, and in the Play Store description where applicable.
